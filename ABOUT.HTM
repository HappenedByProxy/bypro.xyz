About

Abstract, Surreal, Liminal.

I'm a 19 year old student from Wales, studying applied cybersecurity full-time.
I do art as a hobby on the side, and have been doing it since 2019. I use art as an outlet for venting and my imagination.
I make old-school styled 3D renders using an assortment of rather old software, as well as glitch art.
Influenced by the images on 90s/00s drum & bass mixes, I begun my 3D journey on the 5th of September while documenting it on Twitter.

I'm an advocate for a free and open source internet, an archivist of a lot of data and an avid connoisseur of music.

How my 3D art began

It began when I had a bad case of the flu, I was up all night so I went downstairs to try and relax. First, I played some selected ambient works, then, thanks to autoplay, a drum & bass set from 1996 played afterwards.
I fell in love with not only the music, but the images being displayed as the video progressed. The futuristic, utopian aesthetic was irresistable.
Thanks to the Ambiance YouTube channel for saving these otherwise forgotten about sets and mixes.
